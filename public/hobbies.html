Legends of Runeterra

Legends of Runeterra is a strategic digital card game developed by Riot Games. Set in the vibrant world of League of Legends, players construct decks from a wide variety of champions and cards representing the diverse regions of Runeterra.

With its unique gameplay mechanics, including alternating rounds and a dynamic mana system, Legends of Runeterra offers a balanced blend of tactical decision-making, intricate deck-building, and intense battles, providing a fresh and immersive experience for card game enthusiasts.

Magic: the Gathering

Magic: The Gathering, created by mathematics professor Richard Garfield and published by Wizards of the Coast, is an iconic collectible card game that has captivated players for decades. It combines intricate strategy, imaginative lore, and a vast array of cards with diverse abilities and interactions.

Players assume the role of powerful Planeswalkers and engage in thrilling battles by strategically casting spells, summoning creatures, and utilizing various game mechanics. Magic: The Gathering's extensive card pool, deep gameplay mechanics, and competitive scene make it a beloved and enduring staple of the card game community.

Slay the Spire

Slay the Spire is an innovative roguelike deck-building game developed by Mega Crit Games.

In this addictive single-player experience, players ascend the eponymous Spire, facing a series of randomized encounters and challenges on each floor. The game combines the strategic depth of deck-building with turn-based combat, as players collect cards, choose their path, and engage in tactical battles against a diverse range of enemies.

With its compelling progression system, endless replayability, and ever-changing paths, Slay the Spire provides a highly addictive and satisfying experience for fans of both card games and roguelike adventures.

Civilization

Civilization is a legendary turn-based strategy game series developed by Sid Meier and published by Firaxis Games. Players take on the role of a leader guiding a civilization from its humble beginnings to becoming a global powerhouse.

Through diplomacy, exploration, warfare, and technological advancements, players navigate a vast map, build cities, manage resources, conduct research, and shape the course of history. With its deep gameplay, intricate mechanics, and immersive storytelling, Civilization has become synonymous with the 4X (explore, expand, exploit, and exterminate) genre, providing countless hours of strategic and addictive gameplay.

Age of Wonders

Age of Wonders: Age of Wonders is a fantasy turn-based strategy game franchise developed by Triumph Studios. Set in a rich and immersive fantasy world, players assume the role of a leader who must guide their chosen race to greatness.

Combining elements of empire building, tactical combat, and role-playing, Age of Wonders offers a diverse range of units, spells, and heroes for players to utilize in their quest for dominance. With its intricate gameplay, immersive world-building, and strategic depth, Age of Wonders has garnered a dedicated following and is hailed as one of the pillars of the fantasy strategy genre.

Heroes of Might and Magic

Heroes of Might and Magic is a beloved turn-based strategy game series developed by New World Computing. Set in a high fantasy realm, players take on the role of a hero who leads armies, explores the world, and engages in epic battles. The game combines elements of resource management, exploration, and tactical combat, allowing players to recruit troops, capture towns, and develop their heroes' skills and abilities.

With its rich lore, captivating art style, and addictive gameplay, Heroes of Might and Magic has become a staple in the turn-based strategy genre, offering a delightful mix of strategy and adventure.
